Title: Top Quality Arena
Gameplay: The player is dropped into a semi-randomized arena and kill enemies. It is a first person shooter and is a fairly big project. Not complete.
Started as hobby project under summerbreak but turned into school assigment later on.
Created by Albin Fyhr

Features:
- Main Menu
- Settings, includes mouse sens and optimization settings
- Multiple weapons
- Multiple maps
- Map editor, very limited and only supports saving one map
- Two gamemodes, one for hunting coins and one for killing enemies
- Functional UI
- Simple gameplay with decent depth. Semi-randomized maps helps replayability
- Everything handcrafted by me, except music
